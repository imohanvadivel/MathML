MathML

Mathematical Markup Language (MathML) is the W3C recommended way for typesetting mathematical and scientific content on the web, and HTML5 natively supports it.

Unfortunately, only a few browsers, including Firefox and Safari, support MathML natively. For chromium-based browsers, polyfills like MathJax can be used for rendering.
